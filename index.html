MENU

Our offers & deals include;

Neapolitan Pizza

Made with simple and fresh ingredients: basic dough, raw tomatoes, fresh mozzarella, fresh basil and olive oil.

Caprese Pizza

Made up of fresh mozzarella basil and tomatoes and then sprinkled with salt and pepper, drizzled with olive oil and served with some crusty bread.

Florentine Pizza

Has a tender crust, topped with garlic infused olive oil. A mix of creamy ricotta spinach and herbs.

Hawaiian Pizza

Made of tomato sauce, cheese, pineapple, and Canadian bacon or ham. Add peppers, mushrooms, or bacon to this new favorite.

Clazone Pizza

Made of salted bread dough, stuffed with salami, ham or vegetables, mozzarella, ricotta and Parmesan or pecorino cheese, as well as an egg.

Stromboli Pizza

Made with dough, crushed tomatoes, dried oregano and thin slices hot salami and is then rolled into a log and baked.
